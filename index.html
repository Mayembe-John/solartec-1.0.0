About Us

Pioneering a Sustainable Future for African Mobility

X-ENERGI is a new, forward-thinking clean-tech platform set to transform last-mile delivery in Africa. While we are a new company, our foundation is built on an integrated and proven model of "rent-to-own e-motorcycles and smart battery swapping stations," designed specifically for the African market.We are launching with a powerful ecosystem designed for success:

Integrated Ecosystem: We combine high-performance electric motorcycles with a network of smart, solar-ready battery swap stations.

Reliable & Efficient: Our solution directly addresses key market pain points like fuel cost volatility and unreliable grid power.

Partner-Driven Growth: We are building a network of national and channel agents to scale rapidly across Zambia and beyond.a
